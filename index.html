    About Me

    At the age of 18 I moved to the United States from Melbourne, Australia to pursue my childhood dream of becoming a student athlete. I attended Seton Hill University from 2021-2025 taking on roles as captain of the Men’s Soccer team and Head Resident Assistant, allowing me to develop my interpersonal and leadership abilities. As a Data Science major I gained an interdisciplinary background in statistics, machine learning, and mathematics. I am now a graduate student at Stony Brook University pursuing my Master’s in Applied Mathematics & Statistics, concentrating in Quantitative Finance. I am currently interested in applying a variety of statistical methods and modern machine learning techniques to enhance financial decision making. I am working towards a career in Quantitative Finance and this website hopes to give you an insight into my journey to achieving that goal.
